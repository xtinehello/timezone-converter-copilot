U.S. Time Zone Converter (DST Toggle)

Enter a time in one zone and see the impact across U.S. time zones and territories (includes CHST, SST, AST, and Arizona broken out). Uses a manual Standard vs Daylight toggle; zones that don’t observe DST stay fixed.

Inputs
Arizona, AST (PR/USVI), Hawaii, CHST, SST do not change for DST
Day shift shows whether the converted time lands on the previous day, same day, or next day relative to your entered time (without showing a date).
